Background

Born in 2005, Soto stood out from an early age for his ability to learn and excel in various areas. After a successful academic career spanning elementary, middle, high school, and currently college, he has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to excellence. In addition to his dedication to studies, he trained in swimming, soccer, taekwondo, and tennis, honing his physical and tactical skills in each discipline.

In 2022, he faced a great challenge when he suffered a severe collarbone injury during a sporting event. The resulting scar became a symbol of his strength and resilience. This experience taught him to overcome adversity with determination and courage.

Today, Soto continues his path to greatness, combining his academic and physical abilities to face new challenges. His story is one of perseverance and growth, and every day is an opportunity to prove himself in the world that awaits him.
